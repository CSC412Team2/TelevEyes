Welcome to the TelevEyes Project Page!

TelevEyes is an app that keeps track of all the television shows you watch. The app notifies you when the next episode of one of your shows is getting ready to air. It notifies you when the new season of your show or new series of a show is getting ready to premeire. The TelevEyes app also keeps you updated on any changes that could occur during the show's run. You'll receive updates if the show has been cancelled or renewed, changed stations, air dates, and air times.

The individuals working on this project are Khaliq Satchell, Billie Jean Simmons, Joshua Dixon, Nathaniel Richrdson, and TeeTree-O Pittman who all attend Elizabeth City State University. Khaliq Satchell (senior) is a computer science major and the product owner for the project, TelevEyes. Billie Jean Simmons (junior) is a computer science major and the scrum master for the project. Computer science majors Nathaniel Richardson (senior), Joshua Dixon (junior), and TeeTree-O Pittman (junior) are the programmers working on the code for the project.
